Perseverance Rover

Exploring Mars' Jezero Crater to seek signs of ancient life and collect samples for future return to Earth. The most sophisticated rover ever sent to the Red Planet.

The Mars 2020 Perseverance rover mission is part of NASA's Mars Exploration Program, a long-term effort of robotic exploration of the Red Planet.

Mission Objectives

  • Explore geologically diverse landing site
  • Assess ancient habitability
  • Seek signs of ancient life
  • Collect rock samples for future return
  • Demonstrate technology for future exploration
